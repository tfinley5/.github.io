Website Evaluations


Craigslist - Charlotte


Description:


Craiglist is a site that allows people to put up ads, notices, things to sell, etc. for their area. Ex Charlotte


  • C - The only contrast is the blue links against the white background. It doesn't help with visibility because the white and blue are both bright colors and it takes up the whole page.
  • R - The page is covered in repetition but not in a good way. There is no repetition between different types of content instead everything is the same.
  • A - The alignment on the page is quite good. All the columns, title included, are aligned so that everything looks clean.
  • P - Instead of putting all the links on one page, this page is seperated by categories. However, there are so many links that the user can get lost very easily.

IBM


Description:


IBM produces advanced technology and offers programs on their website


  • C - This site uses lighter background so that the information pops out more. Simple colors allow for easy visability
  • R - The use of consistent formatting and spacing creates an organized design for this page.
  • A - Each section of the page is aligned a little differently but each section is aligned and organized
  • P - The page seperates the information displayed by the type of information it is. Theres is even a bar on the side that tells you what section you are in.
