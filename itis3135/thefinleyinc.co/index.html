The Finley Inc.

The Finley Inc. was founded in 2022 and specializes in making clothes meant to withstand the elements. We offer discounted services to first responders and veterans who seek protective gear. With every purchase made, 5% of our profits go toward charities to help disabled veterans and first responders.

About Our Company

  • We support our first responders and veterans
  • Our products can withstand all the elements
  • All products can be fully customized
  • We are a small and family-owned business
  • We offer warranties for products for one year(They should last for 10!)

For our clients, we want to customize our products to fit your needs. We hold ourselves to a standard where where all of our products work for every purpose and you feel comfortable and safe in our products.
